# ISO/IEC 27001:2022

**Published:** October 2022 (Annex A reorganized in line with ISO/IEC 27002:2022)
**Source:** https://www.iso.org/standard/27001
**Pinned version:** 2022

## Controls cited by our detectors

Union of docs/BENCHMARK.md mappings and `framework_refs=[...]` blocks emitted in [`lib/fwrr/detectors/*.py`](https://github.com/ipunithgowda/firewall-review/blob/main/lib/fwrr/detectors/*.py). The citation-verifier compares finding payloads against this list — any Annex A control NOT here is held for QA review.

## Citation format

`{"framework": "ISO_27001_2022", "control_id": "<ID>", "version": "2022"}`

Example: `{"framework": "ISO_27001_2022", "control_id": "A.8.20", "version": "2022"}`

## Citation-verifier behaviour

- Controls listed above: pass.
- Controls NOT listed above: hold for manual review by the QA reviewer.
- Wrong version (e.g. 2013-edition `A.13.1.1` "Network controls"): quarantined.

## Pitfalls (ISO 27001:2022-specific)

- **2013-edition Annex A had 114 controls in 14 domains; 2022 has 93 controls in 4 themes** (Organizational, People, Physical, Technological). Many old IDs RETIRED or merged.
  - 2013 `A.13.1.1` (Network controls) → 2022 `A.8.20` (Networks security)
  - 2013 `A.13.1.2` (Security of network services) → 2022 `A.8.21`
  - 2013 `A.13.1.3` (Segregation in networks) → 2022 `A.8.22`
  - 2013 `A.12.4.x` (Logging and monitoring) → 2022 `A.8.15`–`A.8.17`
  - 2013 `A.10.1.x` (Cryptographic controls) → 2022 `A.8.24`
- **`A.8.23` Web filtering** is NEW in 2022 — does not exist in 2013. Do not back-port.
- **`A.8.9` Configuration management** is NEW in 2022. Detectors that cite it (shadow-rule, duplicate-rule, contradicting-rule) are version-correct only against the 2022 edition.
- **Annex A is normative reference** (not the standard's clauses). Cite Annex A IDs only — never cite ISO/IEC 27002:2022 control numbers, which look identical but live in a different document.
- **No "version 2022.1".** When ISO publishes a corrigendum, it updates the publication date but the control catalogue is stable. Pin `version: "2022"` only.
